How It Works:

  1. We have identified 9 key habits to losing weight sustainably. You start with #1.
  2. You identify if/when you engage in the habit during your average day.
  3. You assess the role this habit plays in your current lifestyle.
  4. If needed, we help you construct a plan to alter this habit. We provide suggestions, tips, and real-world scenarios so you are prepared to attack.
  5. You then spend the week only focused on modifying this one habit. We provide spreadsheets for tracking your progress (if you want to).
  6. At the end of seven days you assess whether you have successfully modified this habit and are ready to move on to the next one.

    7. Over time you lose weight effortlessly as you make small changes to your lifestyle, one at a time. For those who want faster weight-loss, you can attack multiple habits simultaneously.
